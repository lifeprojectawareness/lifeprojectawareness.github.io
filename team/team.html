The life Team

Hannah Estelle Jeanne U. Gopez

Founder of the Life Project

A Psychologies who is assign in Litchfield Penitentiary, New York in the Psychology department was enlighten about mental health in general. She is the founder of THE LIFE PROJECT. She noticed that some young women end up being under her department due to different kinds of mental health problems and eventually to a point of a suicidal person. As a result of care for others Dra. Gopez came up to start this advocacy with a goal of helping, not just the said young women but also teenagers and other people who needs care with regards to mental health.

Mark Luis Galang

Major sponsor of the life project

A Russian businessman who migrated in New York happens to have a daughter who suffered with mental health problem particularly depression. With his lost he then realized that there are ways to prevent this situation and he did not want other parents to suffer like he did. Started from then he funds out different organizations around the city but upon researching about LIFE's goals and objectives he then reached out with organization and became the official and major sponsor of THE LIFE PROJECT

Irah Coleen Quindoza

Ambasadress of the Life Project

A former inmate counselor in Litchfield Penitentiary, New York realized that most of the inmates have mental health problem that needs to be addressed, as much as possible she wanted it without intensive medication involved. With this she supported the advocacy that her co-work in Litchfield Penitentiary started. Ms. Quindoza ‘s devotion and pure intention is what makes her the best ambassadress of THE LIFE PROJECT

Luis Mendoza

Event organizer of the life project

A Communication Arts with major degree in advertising, graduated in Dayanara University, Spain year 2002 who is very interested in helping people with mental health problem. He relates his thesis study with this topic. As he graduated he wanted to give solution with the non-ending negative situation regarding the suicidal people mainly with teenagers. His dedication to help end the suffering of these people that aspire him to became event organizer of LIFE PROJECT. He then also became the new face of THE LIFE PROJECT.
