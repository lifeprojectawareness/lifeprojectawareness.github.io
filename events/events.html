News and Events

Apr 30

Awareness Month

To prevent the increasing number of suicidal people especially teenagers through the use of social media, banners and group of people who promote and support our advocacy. Social Media is trendy specifically to millennials because we are using these hashtags and posts so that we can easily spread the voice of the advocacy’s goal as well as its goal to help the depressed and in pain. We create different kinds and creative banner and put them up in several public places. The awareness month happens every month of September and we supporters wear shirts that are designed by few of our members in order to be uniformed and to show our harmony to the people who suffer from suicidal thoughts.

Jun 17

Listen, Speak & Absorb

Everyone knows that venting out what you actually feel, problems, situations and etc can sooth the pain and make you relief. This event is for someone who needed people to talk to, for someone to feel he/she is not the only one feeling the situation and that if others can they also can. Listen and Speak movement happens in a closed room involving 5-8 people affected by the said negative thoughts and 4-5 members from our group. Each and every one will have the chance to speak of their thoughts and problems. This is similar with an open- forum but listen, speak, absorb movement allows you to absorb and think about the advices and comfort from our people gives to everyone

Sep 21

Mind and Body Boost!

We believe that physical health can affect the way of thinking of an individual. Studies show that physical activities / exercise increases heart rate, which pumps more oxygen to the brain. It also aids the bodily release of a plethora of hormones, all of which participate in aiding and providing a nourishing environment for the growth of brain cells. And so, with “booster to mind and body” which is a sports fest event campaign our objective to help with the use of physical activities is attained. The sports fest’s duration is 3 days including the sports: basketball, volleyball, swimming, football and many more. Every game is only a friendly game and therefore winning or losing is not that important rather focuses on every movement / action of each and every one to successfully achieve the goal.

Nov 04

Spoken Words For The Unspoken Minds

Spoken Poetry is known for expressing a person’s feelings and thoughts creatively, in line with that we strongly believe that it is a good way for people who are upset and depressed to let out their thoughts of suiciding in a creative yet confidential way. The objective is to let them express their emotions without particularly uttering the exact event or person involved. There will be a specific day for this spoken word event. It will be thrice a month with ten days interval. Anyone could come and participate however one must register and follow the given date of their performance. Token of appreciation will be given
